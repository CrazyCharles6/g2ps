The first word is the one given by the respondent from American University in Cairo, the second is the one given by the respondent from a coffeehouse in the working-class neighborhood of Al-Sayida Zaynib.  Items in parentheses were judged to be 'less colloquial' than the others.

In the Arabic column, comma is only used for alternatives; see "Notes" above.
