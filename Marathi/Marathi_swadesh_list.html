Appendix:Indo-Aryan Swadesh lists

Definition from Wiktionary, the free dictionary
Jump to: navigation, search

This is a Swadesh list of Indo-Aryan languages, specifically Sanskrit, Hindi, Urdu, Punjabi, Gujarati, Marathi, Bengali, Kashmiri, Sindhi, Assamese, Sinhalese and Seraiki, compared with that of English.

Transliteration scheme for Urdu is ISO 233 and for other Indo-Aryan languages ISO 15919.
