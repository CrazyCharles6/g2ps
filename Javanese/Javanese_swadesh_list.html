This is a Swadesh list of Austronesian languages, specifically Ilocano, Malay, Tagalog, Cebuano, Kapampangan, Pangasinan, Sasak, Chamorro, Malagasy, Tahitian, Maori, Sundanese, Ibanag, Itawit and Javanese, compared with that of English.

  • Ilocano (Ilokano) (Northern Luzon, Philippines)
  • Malay (Sumatra-Indonesia, Eastern Malaysia, Singapore, Brunei)
  • Tagalog (Southern Luzon, Philippines)
  • Cebuano (Central Visayan Islands, Philippines)
  • Pampangan (Kapampangan) (Central Luzon, Philippines)
  • Pangasinan (Central Luzon, Philippines)
  • Sasak (Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia)
  • Chamorro (Guam and Northern Mariana Islands)
  • Malagasy (Madagascar)
  • Tahitian (Tahiti)
  • Maori (New Zealand)
  • Sundanese (West Java, Indonesia)
  • Ibanag (Northeastern Luzon, Philippines)
  • Itawit (Northeastern Luzon, Philippines)
  • Javanese (Central & East Java, Indonesia)
